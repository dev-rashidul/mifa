Sponsorship

We welcome sponsors to help join our team, promoting team sports for women as a first step to achieving the bigger goals of equality, empowerment, and equity of resources so badly needed at all levels of society, in communities worldwide.

Sponsors need not be companies or organizations linked inherently to athletics or women’s causes – we welcome all corporations, family offices, grass-roots organizations, and individuals to play a part.

Opportunities for recognition

Opportunities for recognition include visual representation (logos on equipment and signage), mentions in communications, as well as press releases, and playing a spotlight role in special events. We know that sponsorship relationships are unique, and the preferences of our partners can vary. Please contact us to explore the options; we guarantee that we’ll together find the perfect arrangement where everyone wins - you, Mamanet, and most importantly, the women whose lives you are directly impacting for the good.
